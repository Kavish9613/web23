How we got here

  1. 2011

    Founded with three weaving families in Chanderi, Madhya Pradesh.

  2. 2015

    Expanded to Kutch's bandhani and ajrakh clusters; first natural-dye line.

  3. 2019

    Launched a fair-wage, pay-on-order program directly with karigars.

  4. 2024

    120 artisan families across seven states. Zero power looms, still.
